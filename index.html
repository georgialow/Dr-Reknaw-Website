AOTEAROA'S MAGNIFICENT SEVEN

With an intoxicating blend of reggae, dub, soul and funk, Wellington seven-piece Dr. Reknaw have developed a live show that fits perfectly with Aotearoa in summer. Dr. Reknaw are proud to announce the release of their lush and uplifting nine-track debut album, Rektified, a vibrational cosmic soup that has been four years in the making. Prior to the Rektified album they began their journey with the release of Earthly Delights.
